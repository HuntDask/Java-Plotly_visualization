The charts help us visualize the various bacterias that we have collected from the volunteers. To start, we use the drop-down menu to select a volunteer's id number . When we are able to find our id, the bar chart will show data from the id we research and the top 10 in that category. The gauge chart gives us the amount of frequency with the number of scrubs per week seen in the chart. The bubbles in the bubble chart show the concentration of the bacteria in the form of how big the bubble is in respect to the concentration.
